    Hostos Game Design Dominates the Climate Game Jam

    amcclure, October 16, 2015May 25, 2016, Competitions, Game Design, News, 1

    Hostos Game Design was well represented at the NYC Climate Game Jam at Barnard College.  Our very own Mark Madison was a member of the team that won first place for their game, entitled Disappearing Island.  Other Hostos participants included Tyrell Bush, Stevie Del Villar, Wilfredo (Kevin) Rodriguez, Shawn Wilson, Jose Deschamps and Brandon Guerra.

    Congratulations to everyone who participated!
